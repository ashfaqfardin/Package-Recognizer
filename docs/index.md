# Packaging Recognizer

An image classification system that identifies and classifies common consumer product packaging into 12 distinct categories.

[**Try the Model Here**](/Package-Recognizer/pkg_recognizer/)

1. Bottled Water
2. Soft Drink Cans
3. Milk Cartons
4. Snack Chips Packets
5. Chocolate Bars
6. Instant Noodles Packets
7. Toothpaste Boxes
8. Shampoo Bottles
9. Soap Bars
10. Cooking Oil Bottles
11. Coffee Jars
12. Detergent Packs
